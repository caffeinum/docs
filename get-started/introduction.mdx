## Welcome to Moai Chain

Moai Chain is the first Layer 2 blockchain that brings native AI capabilities directly into smart contracts. This guide will help you get started with building AI-powered decentralized applications.

## What You'll Learn

- How to connect to Moai Chain
- Deploying your first AI-enabled smart contract
- Using AI functions in your contracts
- Best practices for AI integration

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- Basic knowledge of Solidity and smart contracts
- [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) (v19 or higher)
- [Foundry](https://book.getfoundry.sh/getting-started/installation)
- A wallet with testnet ETH on Optimism

## Development Environment

We recommend using:

- [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com/) with Solidity extension
- [Foundry](https://book.getfoundry.sh/) for contract development
- [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/) for wallet interaction
